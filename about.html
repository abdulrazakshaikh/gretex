ABOUT US

Gretex Share Broking Private Limited was incorporated on 29 April 2010. Since its inception, we have strived to Create maximum liquidity in the market as well as Facilitate buy and sell of securities, in place of investors, for several companies who choose to get listed on the Stock Exchanges, fulfilling our role as a Market Maker and as a Stock Broker, thereby providing quality services to our portfolio of companies. We are a SEBI Registered Market Maker and BSE Registered Stock Broker, working in the industry of Other Financial Intermediary. Under Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Gretex Share Broking is classified as a non-Government company Limited by Shares. Gretex Share Broking is an Unlisted Company.

Is the abbreviation for the word "greatest" which inspires us to become the leader in our field. *Our Logo The letter "T" replicates a tree resembling our attitude to provide financial support to the whole world with both hands. *Our Motto Is to create liquidity in the market as a Market Maker and facilitate buying and selling of securities as a Stock Broker. *Timeline *Incorporated on 29 April 2010 as 'Sherwood Securities Private Limited." *The registered office of company changed from Kolkata, West Bengal to Mumbai, Maharashtra w.e.f 6 January, 2017 vide order dated 21.12.2016 of RegionalDirector (ER) Kolkata. *Name of company changed from Sherwood Securities Private Limited to Gretex Share Broking Private Limited wef 01.09.2017. *Gretex Share Broking did Market Making of 20+ companies as on 29 September,2021.
